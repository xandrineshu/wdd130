Page 2: List of Anime Recommendations ( ⓛ ω ⓛ *)

I would like to warn you that this is a PERSONAL opinion of anime recommendation lists. We all have different tastes when it comes to what we like so please don't hate if your favorite anime isn't in here. After all, I simply am sharing what I THINK is great. I have watched anime since I was younger so I have a pretty good grasp of knowledge when it comes to anime series and movies. Here on this page, I'll be recommending 5 lists, which are: FOR BEGINNERS, POPULAR ANIME, FAMILY NIGHT, BEST ANIME, and ANIME MOVIES.

(There will be only 5 anime per list since there are so much anime that can be in the list but then it will be too long.)

FOR BEGINNERS LIST:

This a list of iconic anime that you must certainly watch to start your journey of anime and as well as getting comfortable in the genre.

POPULAR ANIME LIST:

This is a list of audience-favored anime that rose up in rankings for its story, art, animation, music and etc.

FAMILY NIGHT LIST:

This is a list where you can watch kid-friendly anime with family or/and younger audiences to make your movie watching activities memorable.

BEST ANIME LIST:

This is a personal list of anime that is a must-watch! All of these are my all time favs of all time!!

ANIME MOVIES LIST:

This is a list of some of the best films in anime history that won people hearts and aswell won lots of awards like best animated feature.

Anime isn't a medium that's particularly known for its realism. Superpowers, earth-shattering circumstances, and explosive battles are pretty much the norm in anime. Even some of the most realistic premises can become fantastical and bizarre in this medium. However, there are quite a few anime that are realistic like "A Silent voice" (2016) by Yoshitoki Ōima, "Your Lie in April" (2014) by Naoshi Arakawa, and "Wolf Children" (2012) by Mamoru Hosoda. Not all anime can be fantasy-like! Some can be real tear-jerkers and can pull your heartstrings like it's nothing!
