Now that you have a series you want to watch, where are you going to watch them?
Here are are some of the best platforms to watch your favorite anime for free:

AnimePlanet

Crunchyroll

Hulu

Netflix

Bilibili

Funimation

These are some of the safest and most official websites you can try at home whether it's on your phone, your computer, or your television! When streaming, you get to watch your favorite series anytime and anywhere! （＾・ω・＾✿)

In my opinion, it is best to stream anime than to watch it on television. Of course, you're not limited to following a specific schedule to watch your favorite shows on time. With streaming, you can watch anime online whenever you want. However, television can contain a lot of censorship, which streaming sites remove entirely for a better viewing experience.

WARNING!

Some streaming sites may not have a legal license to stream their shows on their platform so be careful where you watch your anime! While there are no legal consequences to watching anime for free on such sites, they are still illegal.

Sub or Dub anime?

When watching anime, some wonder if it should be watched subbed, or dubbed, and to answer that: It completely depends on personal preference. However, we, anime lovers, prefer the subbed version as it captures the originally intended emotions of the characters, which is often lacking in dubbed versions. Plus, most anime streaming sites may not have dubbed versions of your favorite anime, so the only option is the subbed version.
